## Tools Used

## Dataset
Public retail sales dataset sourced from Kaggle (educational use).

## Methodology
1. Data cleaning and validation
2. Sales aggregation by category and product type
3. Promotion vs non-promotion sales analysis
4. Visualization of sales contribution and trends

## Key Analysis Performed
- Category-wise sales contribution
- Impact of promotions on sales volume
- Product placement and aisle performance
- Sales distribution across product types

## Key Insights
- Jackets contributed ~56% of total sales
- Non-promoted items performed better than promoted items
- Aisle-positioned products generated higher sales
- Seasonal items showed higher demand
